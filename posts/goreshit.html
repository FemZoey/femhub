Goreshit
A review of their music and why do I like their songs.
Goreshit is a british breakcore artist, who has made albums like:

-Overspecialized
-Rituals
-Semantics: The Benzo Chronicles

Among these "Semantics: The Benzo Chronicles" being my favourite,
as it's the album which hosts "Burn this moment into the retina of my eye".
I first found Goreshit thru the rythm game osu which hosts some of his songs
as beatmaps (users can download these and play them), but then I started listening
to his music without it being on osu, and my this artist has some of the best songs I've ever heard,
for the breakcore genre that is.
Their songs make me have a feeling of uneasyness but comfort at the same time, I feel wrapped by the ever so
more violent drum kicks and snares, it feels like all of my worries and issues are leaving my body and I'm becoming one
with the song and it's rythm. This is what makes me like Goreshit, their music is just.. something out of this world, something
scary yet comforting.
Of course there are other breakcore artists like "Svwerslvt", who has a lot of good songs, but their songs don't give me the same feeling
as Goreshit's do.

Anyways thanks for listening to my cringey post about an artist I like!

and remember to stay strong!
